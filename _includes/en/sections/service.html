Notre mission

Bendi&co se propose d'aider tous ceux qui s'aiment et s'apprécient (amoureux sur le point de s'engager, couples, membres de famille, amis, collègues ... ) à apprendre à établir un dialogue constructif.

Dealflow Service

Angelr provides investors with startups within the Nordic startup scene. By becoming a member, Angelr supplies you with a deal flow of companies of your preferences.

Company Presentations

When you find a startup you want to learn more about, you will be invited to a company presentation or we will arrange a private meeting for you. The meetings are a chance to get to know the entrepreneur better, and on presentations you may also connect with other investors.

Legal Assistance

Angelr will help you throughout the entire process when investing in startups. This includes providing legal documents, legal structure for syndication and setting up a holding company.
