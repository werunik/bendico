1. Review & Preperation

The startup gives all necessary information, which is reviewed by Angelr. Appropriate valuation is set. IM and budget is prepared by Angelr and the startup.​​​​​​​

2. Investor Meetings

The startup gives preferences regarding investors and time slots for which they are available to pitch. Angelr scans its network and contact investors with a suitable profile. Investors, who are interested, are booked in for a pitch.

3. Legal Work & Closing

Angelr follows up with investors and sends out a subscription list. If wanted, Angelr can help create all the necessary legal work, including registration and agreements.
